Additional reasearch

During my master's research at Osaka University, I worked in the KOTO Collaboration, studying kaon physics. In addition to the work summarized in my thesis, I conducted research in developing algorithms to improve data analysis. I developed algorithms for event by event noise classification. I processed digital waveforms for separation of overlapping peaks. I also worked with raspberry pi's for data collection with gpios and front-end displays for processed data using PHP and HTML.

I worked primarily in C++ and python, using the ROOT framework.
