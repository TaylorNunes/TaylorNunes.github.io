About me

My name is Taylor and I have a passion for data. Over the past few years I have worked in data analysis while obtaining my master's degree in physics at Osaka University. Before that, I worked as an engineer designing test systems. Recently I have taken a strong interest in machine learning and am working on personal projects with deep learning. I strive to understand the data in the world around us and utilize it to help others. In my free time I deck out my home with home automation gadgets, study Japanese, and go hiking.
